Lasagna

Description

A tender cut of beef is sliced thin, then marinated with lemon juice and soy sauce for at least an hour.

Ingredients

  • New York strip steak
  • lemon
  • white sugar
  • cornstarch
  • onion
  • garlic

Steps

  • Firsly,Place sliced beef in a large bowl. Whisk together lemon juice, soy sauce, sugar, salt, and pepper in a small bowl; pour over beef and toss to coat. Stir in cornstarch. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour to overnight.
  • Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  • Remove beef slices from marinade, shaking to remove any excess liquid. Discard marinade.
  • Heat olive oil in a small skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir onion and garlic in hot oil until onion is golden brown, 5 to 7 minutes; spoon over beef slices.
