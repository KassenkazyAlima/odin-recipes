Description

Fried rice has long been a staple of Aussie takeaway dinners.

Ingredients

    White, long grain rice Soy sauce Oil Peas Chiecken Eggs Carrot

Steps

    Firsly, you will need to wash your rice. Secondly, you should cut your chiecken into pieaces and add soy sauce to it. Thirdly, cut the carrot and potato into mini squares. Cook the chiecken first, then mix it with vegatables.
